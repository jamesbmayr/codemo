What is this?

Chance leads to choice. Options arise out of opportunities. Decisions determine destiny.

These slides depict a thought experiment: alternate timelines where my professional career happened a little differently. Think of this as a choose-your-own-adventure autobiography.

Which ones are real?

After the first few slides - my childhood and education - it branches out into nine intertwined lines. The real events are indicated in blue, while alternate timelines are depicted in red. Each of the alternates has a clear theme, a life I might have lived, and there are always options to move between those paths.

Why these paths?

At the time(line) of this project's creation, I'm a product manager at Mainstay - a communication and engagement platform for higher education institutions. So I focused on plausible paths that might have led me to where I am today, based on jobs and programs I actually applied for, or at least considered at some point.

Perhaps in another timeline, I made it big as a rock band musician, or even published a novel or two. The choices I made and the chances I got open a multiverse of possibilities, only a handful of which are represented here. I encourage you to consider the roads you haven't traveled too!

Who took the photos?

All of these pictures are of me or were taken by me (with some notable exceptions courtesy of Liz Ford). Of course, since I didn't actually do a lot of this, many of these photos are not what they appear!
