Flaggravation

setup

about

This is a vexillological drafting card game with a score-keeping web app.

Players collect components common to flags from all around the world - like colors, structures, and objects often featured on flags - and use these to claim flags shown on screen.

cards

To download and print the cards, visit Flaggravation on DreamHatcher Games.

rules

setup

  1. Open the Flaggravation web app on a phone or tablet.
    • Enter the names of all players.
    • Set a Flag Goal. For first-time players, the recommended goal is 5.
    • Click "Continue". Place the device in a central spot close to all players.
  2. Spread out all the component cards, face-down, in a messy Pool on the table.
  3. Each player selects a Hand of 5 random cards from the Pool.

turn order

Play is simultaneous each round.

gameplay

  1. All players select 1 card from their Hand to place face-down in front of them.
    • At any time, you may look at the cards in front of you.
    • Make sure they don't get mixed up with the Pool.
  2. Once all players have made a selection, all players pass the remaining 4 cards in their Hand to the left (clockwise).
  3. All players now pick up another random card from the Pool to add to their Hand. Play continues in this way throughout the game.

special rules

  1. At any point, if, in your face-down pile in front of you, you have all the components required for a Flag shown on the app, say the name of the Flag aloud.
    • Note that some cards are "Wild" cards, meaning they can represent any 1 of a set of components. (Ex: Colors substitutes for a Red, Yellow, Green, Blue, Black, or White.)
    • If 2 players are eligible (see below), it's whoever says the Flag name first.
  2. Reveal these component cards to show they match the Flag's requirements.
    • If they do not, place these cards back in front of you, face-down.
    • If they do meet the requirements:
      1. Place these component cards back in the Pool, face-down, and mess the Pool around without looking so no one can tell which cards they are.
      2. Additionally, click on the applicable Flag on the app, then select your name. The app will keep track of how many Flags you have collected.
      3. The app also shows how many cards to collect for constructing the Flag. (The longer a Flag goes without being claimed, the more cards it's worth!) Select this quantity of random cards from the Pool to place face-down in front of you.

end

The game ends when someone has claimed the Goal number of Flags; this player wins.
