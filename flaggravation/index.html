Flaggravation

setup

about

This is a vexillological drafting card game with a score-keeping web app.

Players collect components common to flags from all around the world - like colors, structures, and objects often featured on flags - and use these to claim flags shown on screen.

cards

To download and print the cards, visit Flaggravation on DreamHatcher Games.

rules

setup

  1. Open the Flaggravation web app on a phone or tablet.
    • Enter the names of all players.
    • Set a Flag Goal. For first-time players, the recommended goal is 5.
    • Click "Continue". Place the device in a central spot close to all players.
  2. Shuffle the component cards and place them in a face-down Draw Deck.

turn order

Play is simultaneous each round.

gameplay

  1. All players take cards from the Draw Deck to get to a Hand of 5 cards.
  2. All players place 1 Hand card face-up in front of them in a Personal Pile.
  3. After all players have made a selection and confirmed they're ready, all players pass the remaining cards in their Hand to the left (clockwise).
  4. Play continues in this way throughout the game.

special rules

  1. After placing your card, but before passing your Hand to the next player, you may claim a Flag by saying its name aloud. Your Personal Pile must contain the components required for the Flag, as shown on the app.
    • Some cards are "Wild" and represent any 1 of a set of components. (Ex: Colors substitutes for a Red, Yellow, Green, Blue, Black, or White.)
    • If 2 players have a valid claim, whoever said the Flag name first proceeds.
  2. Consolidate the component cards to show they match the Flag's requirements:
    • Place these component cards in a face-up Discard Deck.
    • In the app, next to the Flag, is a number of bonus cards (0, 1, or 2). Select this many cards from your Hand to place in your Personal Pile.
    • In the app, select the Flag, and then your name, to increase your score.
  3. When the Draw Deck runs out, shuffle the Discard Deck to make a new one.

end

The game ends when someone has claimed the Goal number of Flags; this player wins.
