In this competitive worker placement game, you take on the role of fruit farmers, cultivating a grove of citrus trees like lemons, limes, oranges, and grapefruit.

Use your trees to grow more exotic cultivars, like yuzuquats and tangelos.
Or harvest the fruit to make smoothies, jam, and candy.

Build your citrus business and corner the fruit market first!
